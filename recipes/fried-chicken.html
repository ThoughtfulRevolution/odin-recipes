Fried Chickoor

All hail Fried Chickoor!

As it rose from the deep fryer, Fried Chickoor has been omnipresent as an ethereal part of every restaurant, diner, and pub's ethos. The deity can be summoned with offerings of lettuce, cucumber, and any other yield harvested from throughout the year. Much like a seance, when your family is ready to eat you will gather around the dinner table in order to summon Fried Chickoor in all its glory!

Ingredients

2 Gallons of canola oil

1 Large pot

7 Candles

Salt; measured with your heart

Pepper; measured with your soul

Bountiful harvest from the year*

1 Appetite per person**


*Fried Chickoor prefers green vegetables

**Fried Chickoor can smell your hunger


Steps

  1. Grab the large pot and add 2 gallons of canola oil to it
  2. Light all 7 candles and place them in a circle around the table.
    • Please note, this step is for your protection in the event that Fried Chickoor attempts to leave the dining area. Proceed at your own risk.
  3. Place the pot in the middle of the dinner table and set the harvest next to the pot, Carefully!
  4. Have the patriarch of the family slowly and continuously add the salt to the pot with their heart.
  5. Have the matriarch of the family slowly and continuously add the pepper to the pot with their soul.
  6. As the pot begins to effervesce, have other family members rapidly throw the entirety of the harvest into the pot.
  7. Once all harvest has been added, all members must stop, sit still, and close their eyes.
    • Ignore any sounds you might hear coming from the pot as this is when Fried Chickoor will slowly crawl out of the pot and grace you with its presence. Do Not leave the circle under any circumstances.
  8. Once all shaking has ceased and the voices have subsided, open your eyes and feast upon Fried Chickoor!
    • Pairs great with Chablis or Riesling

Serves 5-8
